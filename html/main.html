Welcome to Rose Lock!

About Us

Rose Lock is a door lock system that implements facial recognition, python, and maglock to secure a safe home.

Founded by 4 undergraduate students at UCR, we aim to create a safer place for everyone.

To learn more, please watch the video on the left!

Objective

Safer and Handy Door Lock System

Tired of losing your keys and worried about your door security?

Rose Lock allows the user to open a desired door online, whenever and wherever they want using our website. Whenever the user succesfully logins to our interface using the login button, they will be sent to a Turn On/Off page for the maglock integrated into a physical door. Because our door has no keylock compared to a regular door, our door will only open when the user turns on the maglock and the camera on the door recognizes the user; This provides security and safety for the user. Since the user can open the door anytime online, they also would not need a physical key, never having to worry about accidentally losing it!
